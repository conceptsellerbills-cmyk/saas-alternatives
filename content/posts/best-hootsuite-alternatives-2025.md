# Best Hootsuite Alternatives 2025: Cheaper Social Media Management Tools

Hootsuite was once the default choice for social media management — but a 2023 pricing overhaul that eliminated the free plan and raised professional plans to $99/month ($249/month for Team) sent thousands of users searching for alternatives. Today, better social media management tools exist at every price point, including genuinely capable free options.

---

## Why Marketers Are Leaving Hootsuite

- **Free plan eliminated** in July 2023
- **Price jumped from $49 to $99/month** for the Professional plan
- **Team plan jumped from $179 to $249/month**
- **Interface feels dated** compared to newer tools
- **Analytics limited** to 30-day lookback on lower plans
- **Organic engagement tools** removed from lower tiers

---

## Top 8 Hootsuite Alternatives for 2025

### 1. Buffer — Best Clean & Affordable Alternative

**Price:** Free (3 channels); Essentials $6/channel/month

Buffer is the simplest, most focused social media scheduling tool available. It does one thing — scheduling and publishing — and does it exceptionally well. The interface is clean, the mobile app is excellent and the pricing is fair.

**Key features:**
- Schedule posts across all major platforms
- Browser extension for one-click scheduling from anywhere
- AI Assistant for caption generation and repurposing
- Analytics with engagement metrics and best time to post
- Link in bio tool (Start Page) included free
- Instagram Stories and Reels scheduling
- Team collaboration with draft approvals

**Best for:** Freelancers, small businesses and individuals who want simple, reliable scheduling without the complexity.

---

### 2. Later — Best for Visual Content and Instagram

**Price:** Free (1 social set, 30 posts/month); Growth from $25/month

Later is built around a visual content calendar — you drag and drop media into a grid that shows exactly how your Instagram feed will look. Its Instagram-first philosophy has evolved to include all major platforms.

**Key features:**
- Visual grid planner for Instagram feed preview
- Media library with tagging and search
- Link in Bio page with analytics
- Hashtag analytics and suggestions
- Best time to post recommendations
- User-generated content collection
- First comment scheduling for hashtags

**Best for:** Brands with strong visual identities — especially fashion, food, travel and lifestyle.

---

### 3. Sprout Social — Best Enterprise Alternative

**Price:** From $249/seat/month (Standard)

Sprout Social is Hootsuite's true enterprise competitor. Its listening tools, CRM integration and team workflow features are best-in-class — but the price reflects this. If your team needs the most advanced social media management available, Sprout is worth the cost.

**Key features:**
- Social listening across platforms and the web
- Built-in CRM with contact history
- Shared inbox for team collaboration on DMs
- Campaign tagging and performance attribution
- Competitor benchmarking and industry reports
- Employee advocacy platform (Bambu)
- Salesforce and HubSpot integrations

**Best for:** Enterprise marketing teams with large social media operations and compliance requirements.

---

### 4. Metricool — Best Free Analytics Alternative

**Price:** Free (unlimited history, 1 brand); Starter $22/month

Metricool offers something rare: a genuinely capable free plan with unlimited historical data. Its analytics depth rivals paid tools, including competitor analysis, hashtag tracking and detailed engagement metrics.

**Key features:**
- Unified dashboard for all social platforms
- Competitor analysis and benchmarking
- Best time to post based on your actual audience data
- Google Ads and Facebook Ads analytics
- Twitch and YouTube analytics
- Link in bio tool included
- White-label reports for agencies

**Best for:** Marketers who need deep analytics and competitor tracking without paying Hootsuite prices.

---

### 5. Planable — Best for Teams and Agencies

**Price:** Free (50 posts total); Basic $39/workspace/month

Planable is built around content collaboration and approval workflows. Its interface shows social posts exactly as they'll appear when published, making it easy for clients to review and approve content before it goes live.

**Key features:**
- Real-time collaboration with comments per post
- Multi-level approval workflows (reviewer → manager → client)
- Preview posts as they'll appear on each platform
- Grid view for Instagram feed planning
- Unlimited workspaces on agency plans
- Guest access for clients (no account required)

**Best for:** Agencies managing multiple clients who need a clean approval workflow.

---

### 6. SocialBee — Best for Evergreen Content Recycling

**Price:** From $29/month

SocialBee's standout feature is its category-based content recycling — you organize posts into categories (promotional, educational, curated) and set how often each category should post. This keeps your queue full automatically.

**Key features:**
- Category-based scheduling with recycling
- Content curation via RSS feeds and URLs
- Canva integration for in-app design
- Post variations to avoid repetition
- Audience growth tools
- Detailed analytics per category
- Agency features with workspace separation

**Best for:** Solopreneurs and small businesses that want to maintain consistent posting without constant manual effort.

---

### 7. Publer — Best Value Multi-Platform Tool

**Price:** Free (3 accounts, limited); Professional $12/month

Publer offers LinkedIn carousels, TikTok scheduling and even Google Business Profile posting — a combination many competitors don't support. The AI writing assistant (powered by GPT-4) is included in paid plans.

**Key features:**
- LinkedIn carousel creator
- TikTok and YouTube scheduling
- Google Business Profile posts
- AI caption and hashtag generator
- Watermarking for images
- Browser extension for content curation
- RSS auto-posting

**Best for:** Small businesses that need wide platform coverage at minimal cost.

---

### 8. Zoho Social — Best for Zoho Users

**Price:** Free (1 brand, 1 team member); Standard $15/month

Zoho Social integrates directly with Zoho CRM, letting you attribute social media interactions to leads and contacts. If you're in the Zoho ecosystem, it's the logical social media choice.

**Key features:**
- Smart queue with best time to post optimization
- CRM sync — social interactions appear on contact records
- Custom reporting and white-label reports
- Brand monitoring and keyword alerts
- Team collaboration with roles
- zSHARE browser extension

**Best for:** Businesses using Zoho CRM who want social media data connected to their sales pipeline.

## How to Choose

**Choose Buffer if:** You want simple, affordable scheduling that just works.

**Choose Metricool if:** Analytics and competitor benchmarking are more important than publishing features.

**Choose Planable if:** You manage social for clients and need a clean approval process.

**Choose Later if:** Instagram is your primary channel and visual planning matters.

## FAQ

**Is there a free Hootsuite replacement?**
Buffer (3 channels), Metricool (1 brand, unlimited history) and Later (30 posts/month) all offer useful free plans. Metricool has the strongest free analytics tier.

**Which tool handles TikTok scheduling?**
Publer, Later, Metricool, Buffer and SocialBee all support TikTok scheduling. Coverage varies — check which features are available for TikTok vs. other platforms.

**Can I import my Hootsuite data?**
Most scheduling content can be exported as CSV and imported manually. Scheduled posts, analytics and historical data generally don't transfer between platforms.
