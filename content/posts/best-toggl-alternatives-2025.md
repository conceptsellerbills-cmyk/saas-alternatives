# Best Toggl Alternatives 2025: Free & Cheaper Time Tracking Tools

Toggl Track is one of the most widely used time tracking tools, loved for its simple one-click timer and clean reporting. Its free plan covers individual users well, but team pricing at $9/user/month (Starter) and $18/user/month (Premium) adds up quickly for growing teams — especially agencies with large headcounts.

This guide covers the best Toggl alternatives for 2025, from free open source options to enterprise time tracking platforms.

---

## Why Teams Look for Toggl Alternatives

- **Team plans get expensive** — $9–$18/user/month for 20 users is $180–$360/month
- **Billable rates and invoicing** require higher plans
- **Client billing features** are basic compared to dedicated billing tools
- **No payroll integration** on lower plans
- **Limited project budgeting** tools on the Starter plan

---

## Top 8 Toggl Alternatives for 2025

### 1. Clockify — Best Free Toggl Alternative

**Price:** Free (unlimited users, unlimited tracking); paid from $4.99/user/month

Clockify is the most popular free time tracking tool and the strongest direct Toggl alternative. Its free plan includes unlimited users, unlimited projects and unlimited time entries — with no payment required. This makes it dramatically more affordable than Toggl for teams.

**Key features:**
- Unlimited users, projects and clients (free)
- One-click timer with manual entry option
- Browser extensions and desktop apps
- Detailed reports by user, project, client and tag
- Billable rates and invoice generation (paid)
- Attendance tracking and leave management (paid)
- Kiosk mode for shared device clock-in
- Integrations with Asana, Trello, Jira, GitHub

**Best for:** Teams of any size that need time tracking without per-user licensing costs.

---

### 2. Harvest — Best for Invoicing Integration

**Price:** Free (1 user, 2 projects); Pro $12/user/month

Harvest is the gold standard for time tracking plus invoicing. Its invoice generation directly from tracked time, automatic payment reminders and Stripe/PayPal integration make it the favorite for agencies and freelancers who bill by the hour.

**Key features:**
- One-click invoice generation from tracked time
- Online payment collection via Stripe and PayPal
- Automatic payment reminders
- Budget tracking with overage alerts
- Expense tracking with receipt photos
- Team capacity planning view
- Integrations with Asana, Basecamp, QuickBooks, Xero

**Best for:** Agencies and freelancers that need time-to-invoice in one workflow.

---

### 3. TimeCamp — Best for Automatic Time Tracking

**Price:** Free (unlimited users, basic); paid from $3.99/user/month

TimeCamp's standout feature is automatic time tracking — it silently monitors which applications and websites you use and categorizes time automatically. This makes it perfect for knowledge workers who forget to start timers.

**Key features:**
- Automatic time tracking via app and website monitoring
- Project and task-based time tracking
- Billable hours and invoicing
- Budget tracking with profit/cost analysis
- Attendance and leave management
- Integrations with 100+ apps
- Computer activity timeline for review

**Best for:** Knowledge workers and agencies that want accurate time data without manual tracking discipline.

---

### 4. Kimai — Best Free Open Source Alternative

**Price:** Free (self-hosted); cloud from €10/month

Kimai is an open source time tracking application that you host yourself. It's been actively developed since 2006 and offers a comprehensive feature set including multi-currency billing, client portals and LDAP authentication.

**Key features:**
- Full-featured time tracking with project and activity structure
- Multi-user with roles and permissions
- Invoicing with custom templates
- Customer portal for time and invoice review
- LDAP/Active Directory integration
- REST API for custom integrations
- Plugin ecosystem for extensions
- Available as Docker container for easy deployment

**Best for:** Technical teams that want self-hosted time tracking with zero licensing fees.

---

### 5. Everhour — Best for Project Management Integration

**Price:** Free (5 users); paid from $10/user/month

Everhour embeds directly inside project management tools — adding time tracking natively into Asana, Jira, Trello, GitHub, Basecamp and Linear without requiring a tab switch. If your team already lives in these tools, Everhour is the most seamless option.

**Key features:**
- Native integration inside Asana, Jira, Trello, GitHub, Basecamp, Linear
- Budget tracking per project with alerts
- Resource planning and capacity management
- Client billing with invoice generation
- Expense tracking
- Team scheduling view
- Chrome extension for any web-based tool

**Best for:** Teams using Asana, Jira or other project management tools who want time tracking built into existing workflows.

---

### 6. Hubstaff — Best for Remote Team Monitoring

**Price:** From $7/user/month (Starter)

Hubstaff goes beyond time tracking to include activity monitoring, GPS location tracking, screenshots and productivity scoring. This makes it controversial — but for field service teams and remote contractors, it provides accountability that simple timers can't.

**Key features:**
- Time tracking with optional screenshot capture
- Activity levels based on keyboard/mouse activity
- GPS location tracking for field teams
- Geofencing for automatic clock-in/out
- Payroll integration with automatic payments
- Project budgets and client invoicing
- Job site attendance reports

**Best for:** Field service companies, construction teams and businesses that manage remote contractors.

---

### 7. Timeular — Best Hardware + Software Alternative

**Price:** $9/user/month (includes free physical tracker device)

Timeular takes a unique physical approach — an 8-sided tracker device that sits on your desk. Flip it to a different side to start tracking a different activity. The tactile interaction reduces friction significantly for users who forget to use software timers.

**Key features:**
- Physical 8-sided tracking device (free with subscription)
- Automatic time suggestions based on calendar and app usage
- Detailed reporting by project, client and activity
- Billable rates and invoice generation
- Leave and overtime tracking
- Team analytics and productivity insights
- Works alongside manual digital entry

**Best for:** Individual professionals who find software timers easy to forget and want a physical reminder.

---

### 8. Paymo — Best for Project + Time Bundled

**Price:** Free (1 user); paid from $9.90/user/month

Paymo combines project management, time tracking, billing and resource planning in one platform. If you're currently using separate tools for project management and time tracking, Paymo consolidates both.

**Key features:**
- Kanban boards, Gantt charts and task lists
- Time tracking built into every task
- Project estimates vs. actual time comparison
- Client invoicing from tracked time
- Expense tracking and profitability analysis
- Resource management and workload view
- Client portal for project visibility

**Best for:** Small agencies and freelancers that want project management and time tracking in a single tool.

**Choose Clockify if:** You need free unlimited time tracking for a team — no tool matches this value.

**Choose Harvest if:** Invoicing from time entries is critical to your workflow.

**Choose Kimai if:** You want self-hosted time tracking with zero ongoing costs.

## FAQ

**Is there a completely free time tracker for teams?**
Clockify's free plan includes unlimited users, unlimited projects and unlimited tracking. Kimai is completely free when self-hosted. Both are legitimate Toggl alternatives for budget-conscious teams.

**Can I export time data from Toggl?**
Yes — Toggl allows CSV and PDF exports of all time entries. Most alternatives can import via CSV. The main migration effort is recreating project and client structures.

**Which tool is best for freelancer invoicing?**
Harvest is the strongest option for hourly invoicing — its invoice generation from tracked time with online payment collection is the most seamless workflow available.
