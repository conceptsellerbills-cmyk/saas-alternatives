# Best HubSpot Alternatives 2025: 8 CRMs That Cost Less and Do More

HubSpot is powerful, but the pricing is notoriously confusing and can escalate quickly. Marketing Hub Professional starts at $800/month. Sales Hub Pro is $100/user/month. A fully-featured HubSpot setup for a small team can easily exceed $2,000/month.

Here are 8 HubSpot alternatives that offer comparable functionality at lower cost — plus some that beat HubSpot in specific areas.

## Best HubSpot Alternatives

### 1. Salesforce — Best Enterprise CRM
The most powerful CRM platform available. More complex, more expensive than HubSpot at similar tiers, but deeper customization and integration possibilities. Industry standard for mid-to-large enterprises.
**Price:** $25-300/user/month | **Best for:** Large enterprises, complex sales processes

### 2. Pipedrive — Best for Sales-Focused Teams
Pipeline-first CRM designed for salespeople. Intuitive deal management, activity tracking, and automation. Less marketing automation than HubSpot but excellent for pure sales CRM at a fraction of the price.
**Price:** $15-99/user/month | **Best for:** Sales teams who don't need marketing automation

### 3. Zoho CRM — Best Value
Comprehensive CRM with sales, marketing, customer service, and analytics. Competitive with HubSpot's features at dramatically lower pricing.
**Price:** Free (3 users); $20-65/user/month | **Best for:** SMBs wanting full CRM suite at lower cost

### 4. ActiveCampaign — Best for Marketing Automation
Email marketing + CRM + automation in one platform. Better marketing automation than HubSpot at its price point — visual automation builder, predictive sending, and deep segmentation.
**Price:** $15-259/month | **Best for:** Email marketing-first businesses

### 5. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — Best Free Email/CRM
Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day free. SMS marketing included. CRM functionality in paid plans. Excellent for small businesses needing email marketing + basic CRM.
**Price:** Free; $25-65/month | **Best for:** Small businesses, email marketing focus

### 6. Notion + Tally + Make — Best No-Code CRM
Build a custom CRM in Notion (database), collect leads with Tally forms, automate with Make. Total cost: ~$30/month vs $800/month for HubSpot Marketing Pro.
**Best for:** Technical teams willing to build, startups with simple CRM needs

### 7. Attio — Best Modern CRM UX
The most beautiful, modern CRM interface available. Data model is fully flexible — customize objects, relationships, and workflows. Gaining rapid adoption among tech-forward teams.
**Price:** Free; $34/user/month | **Best for:** Modern teams who prioritize UX

### 8. HighLevel — Best for Agencies
All-in-one platform for agencies: CRM, email marketing, SMS, landing pages, review management, client portals. White-label options.
**Price:** $97/month flat (unlimited clients) | **Best for:** Marketing agencies

## When to Stick With HubSpot

HubSpot remains the best choice if:
- You're on their free CRM (genuinely the best free CRM available)
- Your team is deeply trained on HubSpot and switching costs are high
- You need the specific HubSpot ecosystem (app marketplace, Breeze AI, ops hub features)
- You're in a growth phase where the all-in-one convenience outweighs the cost

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is HubSpot free CRM still worth it in 2025?
Yes — HubSpot's free CRM is genuinely excellent and one of the best deals in SaaS. The pricing problems start when you need professional features.

### What's the easiest HubSpot alternative to switch to?
Pipedrive for sales teams (easy migration), ActiveCampaign for marketing teams. Both have HubSpot import tools.

### Can I use multiple tools instead of HubSpot?
Yes — many teams use Pipedrive for CRM + ActiveCampaign for email + Zapier to connect them. This "best of breed" approach is often cheaper than HubSpot and allows choosing the best tool for each function.
