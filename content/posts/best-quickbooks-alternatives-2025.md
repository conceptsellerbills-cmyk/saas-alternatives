# Best QuickBooks Alternatives 2025: Cheaper Accounting Software

QuickBooks Online has dominated small business accounting for decades — but a series of aggressive price increases has pushed many users to explore alternatives. The Simple Start plan now costs $35/month, and the Plus plan (needed for inventory and project tracking) runs $90/month. Add payroll and you're easily spending $150–$200/month just on accounting software.

Here are the best QuickBooks alternatives for 2025, covering everything from free open source bookkeeping tools to full-featured cloud accounting platforms.

---

## Why Businesses are Leaving QuickBooks

- **Constant price increases** — QuickBooks Online prices have risen 30–50% in the past 3 years
- **Forced cloud migration** from QuickBooks Desktop (which is being discontinued)
- **Sluggish performance** — browser-based QuickBooks can be frustratingly slow
- **Limited users** on lower tiers (1 user on Simple Start, 3 on Essentials)
- **Payroll is expensive** — the payroll addon costs an additional $45–$125/month

---

## Top 8 QuickBooks Alternatives for 2025

### 1. FreshBooks — Best for Freelancers & Service Businesses

**Price:** From $19/month

FreshBooks is purpose-built for self-employed professionals and small service businesses. Its invoicing experience is significantly more polished than QuickBooks, and the time tracking and project features are excellent.

**Key features:**
- Professional invoicing with automated payment reminders
- Time tracking with project profitability reports
- Expense tracking with receipt scanning
- Double-entry accounting with P&L and balance sheets
- Client retainer management
- Integrates with Stripe, PayPal, Gusto

**Best for:** Consultants, freelancers, agencies and service businesses.

---

### 2. Xero — Best QuickBooks Alternative for Growing Businesses

**Price:** From $15/month (Starter); $42/month (Standard)

Xero is QuickBooks' strongest direct competitor globally, particularly popular in Australia, UK and Canada. Its multi-currency support, strong bank reconciliation tools and clean interface make it a favorite for businesses with international operations.

**Key features:**
- Unlimited users on all plans (vs. QuickBooks' user limits)
- Real-time bank feed reconciliation
- Multi-currency accounting (160+ currencies)
- Inventory tracking and purchase orders
- Payroll (via Gusto integration in the US)
- 1,000+ app integrations

**Best for:** Growing businesses, those with international clients, and accountant-led operations.

---

### 3. Wave — Best Free QuickBooks Alternative

**Price:** Free (accounting + invoicing); payroll and payments are paid

Wave is remarkable in that it offers double-entry accounting, invoicing, and receipt scanning completely free — with no transaction or user limits. Revenue comes from payment processing and payroll.

**Key features:**
- Full double-entry accounting (free)
- Unlimited invoices and customers (free)
- Income and expense tracking (free)
- Financial reports: P&L, balance sheet, cash flow
- Receipt scanning via mobile app
- Payroll: $20/month + $6/employee

**Best for:** Freelancers, sole proprietors and very small businesses that need real accounting without the subscription.

---

### 4. Zoho Books — Best Value All-in-One

**Price:** Free (under $50K revenue); paid from $15/month

Zoho Books offers surprisingly deep accounting features at a very competitive price. Its tight integration with the Zoho suite (CRM, Inventory, Payroll, Analytics) makes it especially attractive if you're building a Zoho-based business stack.

**Key features:**
- Full accounting with multi-currency support
- Invoicing, quotes, purchase orders
- Project billing and time tracking
- Inventory management (Zoho Inventory integration)
- GST/VAT compliance tools for international businesses
- Client portal for invoice approval and payment

**Best for:** Small to mid-size businesses, especially those already using Zoho CRM.

---

### 5. Sage 50 — Best QuickBooks Desktop Replacement

**Price:** From $58.92/month

For businesses that rely on QuickBooks Desktop and are resisting the cloud migration, Sage 50 (formerly Peachtree) offers the most comparable desktop-first experience with optional cloud access.

**Key features:**
- Robust desktop accounting with cloud backup
- Advanced inventory management
- Job costing and project accounting
- Multi-company and multi-currency support
- Strong audit trail and compliance features
- US payroll with tax table updates

**Best for:** Established businesses that prefer desktop accounting with strong inventory and job costing.

---

### 6. GnuCash — Best Free Open Source Alternative

**Price:** Free (open source)

GnuCash is a professional-grade open source accounting application with double-entry bookkeeping, bank reconciliation and financial reporting. It lacks cloud access and collaboration features, but for single-user bookkeeping it's extremely capable.

**Key features:**
- Full double-entry bookkeeping
- Bank and credit card reconciliation
- Invoicing and bill payment
- Multiple currency support
- Business reporting (P&L, balance sheet, trial balance)
- Scheduled transactions

**Best for:** Solo business owners and freelancers comfortable with desktop software who want zero licensing cost.

---

### 7. Holded — Best for European Businesses

**Price:** From $14/month

Holded is a modern cloud ERP designed with European compliance (VAT, SII, Intrastat) at its core. It combines accounting, CRM, invoicing and project management in a clean interface.

**Key features:**
- Full accounting with European tax compliance
- Invoicing in multiple formats
- CRM and pipeline management
- Inventory and warehouse management
- Team and project management
- Payroll module (Spain)

**Best for:** Small European businesses that need VAT compliance and don't want US-centric software.

---

### 8. Odoo Accounting — Best Open Source Enterprise Alternative

**Price:** Free (community, self-hosted); $31.10/user/month (cloud)

Odoo is a full ERP suite with a powerful accounting module. The community edition is open source and free to self-host, making it a realistic QuickBooks alternative for businesses that want enterprise features without enterprise pricing.

**Key features:**
- Full accounting with AI-powered bank reconciliation
- Invoicing and vendor bills
- Multi-company, multi-currency support
- Expense management and reporting
- Tight integration with Odoo CRM, Inventory, Payroll
- Automatic tax computation for 50+ countries

**Best for:** Technical teams and growing businesses that want a scalable ERP with zero licensing costs.

## How to Choose the Right QuickBooks Alternative

**Choose Wave if:** You're a freelancer or very small business that needs real accounting for free.

**Choose Xero if:** You have international clients, multiple team members, or your accountant already uses Xero.

**Choose FreshBooks if:** Invoicing and time tracking are your primary needs (service business).

**Choose Odoo if:** You need a full ERP and are comfortable with self-hosting.

## FAQ

**Can I import my QuickBooks data?**
Yes. Most alternatives accept QuickBooks exports via IIF or CSV. Xero, FreshBooks and Wave have migration guides. Chart of accounts and transaction history transfer well; custom reports need to be rebuilt.

**Is Wave really free?**
Yes — the accounting and invoicing features are genuinely free with no limits. Wave earns revenue from optional payment processing (2.9% + 30¢) and payroll.

**Which alternative is best for an accountant-managed business?**
Xero is the accountant's favorite globally. Most accounting firms are familiar with it and offer fixed-fee packages for Xero clients.

**Does anything replace QuickBooks Desktop?**
Sage 50 is the closest equivalent. Odoo Community (self-hosted) is a powerful option for businesses that want modern software without cloud dependency.

## Conclusion

QuickBooks remains competent accounting software, but its pricing model now works against small businesses. Wave offers real accounting for free. Xero matches QuickBooks Plus features at a lower price with unlimited users. FreshBooks wins for service businesses that prioritize invoicing. For zero-cost accounting with full data control, GnuCash and Odoo Community are proven alternatives used by millions of businesses worldwide.
