# Best Teachable Alternatives 2025: Cheaper Online Course Platforms

Teachable is one of the most popular platforms for selling online courses — but its pricing structure has frustrated creators since it started. The free plan takes a 10% transaction fee (now reduced to 10% but still significant), and the Pro plan at $119/month is required to remove the fee entirely and access graded quizzes, course completion certificates and affiliate marketing.

Here are the best Teachable alternatives for 2025 across different creator needs and budgets.

---

## Why Creators Look for Teachable Alternatives

- **Transaction fees** on lower plans eat into revenue
- **$119/month Pro plan** required for most professional features
- **Limited customization** for course pages and student experience
- **No community features** built in (requires third-party tools)
- **Email marketing requires Teachable's own paid tool** or external integration

---

## Top 7 Teachable Alternatives for 2025

### 1. Thinkific — Best Free Teachable Alternative

**Price:** Free (1 course, unlimited students, no transaction fees); paid from $49/month

Thinkific's free plan is significantly more generous than Teachable's — no transaction fees, unlimited students and one course with all core features. The paid plans unlock multiple courses, communities and advanced customization at competitive prices.

**Key features:**
- No transaction fees on any plan (including free)
- Unlimited students on all plans
- Quizzes, surveys and assignments
- Drip content scheduling
- Community features (paid plans)
- Student certificates
- Stripe and PayPal payments
- Membership and subscription pricing
- Zapier integration for automation

**Best for:** New course creators who want to start without transaction fees or monthly costs.

---

### 2. Podia — Best for Creators Who Sell Multiple Products

**Price:** From $39/month (Mover); $89/month (Shaker — no transaction fees)

Podia combines courses, digital downloads, memberships, webinars and coaching in one platform. If you sell multiple types of digital products, Podia's all-in-one approach eliminates the need for multiple tools.

**Key features:**
- Online courses with video hosting
- Digital downloads (ebooks, templates, software)
- Memberships with recurring billing
- Webinars (Shaker plan)
- Email marketing built in (no external tool needed)
- Affiliate program
- Live chat for student support
- Unlimited everything on paid plans

**Best for:** Digital creators who sell a mix of courses, downloads and memberships.

---

### 3. Kajabi — Best Premium All-in-One Platform

**Price:** From $149/month (Basic); $199/month (Growth)

Kajabi is the premium alternative to Teachable — combining courses, communities, email marketing, landing pages, podcasts and coaching in a single platform. It's more expensive but eliminates the need for multiple SaaS subscriptions.

**Key features:**
- Online courses with rich media support
- Community platform (like a private Facebook group)
- Email marketing with automation sequences
- Landing pages and sales funnels
- Pipeline builder for sales automation
- Podcast hosting
- Coaching and appointment booking
- Mobile app for students

**Best for:** Established creators earning $5K+/month who want to consolidate tools and invest in premium infrastructure.

---

### 4. Gumroad — Best for Simple Digital Product Sales

**Price:** Free; 10% transaction fee (reduces with volume)

Gumroad is the simplest way to sell digital products including courses. There's no monthly fee — just a transaction fee that decreases as you sell more. For creators just starting out or with low volume, the zero fixed-cost model is attractive.

**Key features:**
- No monthly subscription
- Courses, ebooks, memberships, software
- Discover marketplace for organic traffic
- Email list built in
- Affiliate program
- Pay-what-you-want pricing
- Subscription products
- Overlay checkout (embed in your own site)

**Best for:** Creators who want zero fixed costs and are comfortable with transaction fees.

---

### 5. LearnWorlds — Best for Interactive Learning

**Price:** From $29/month (Starter, $5/course fee); $99/month (Pro Trainer)

LearnWorlds focuses on interactive learning experiences — with video annotations, built-in assessments, SCORM support and white-label mobile apps. If learning outcomes matter more than quick course creation, LearnWorlds delivers the most sophisticated student experience.

**Key features:**
- Interactive video with clickable hotspots
- Built-in assessments with 16 question types
- SCORM and xAPI compliance for corporate training
- White-label mobile app (paid addon)
- Community features with social learning
- Certificates with custom designs
- Affiliate marketing
- Multi-instructor support

**Best for:** Corporate trainers, coaches and educators who prioritize learning engagement over simple content delivery.

---

### 6. Circle + Course Platform — Best Community-First Approach

**Price:** Circle from $89/month; combine with any course platform

For creators where community is as important as the course itself, Circle is the best standalone community platform. Combine it with a simple course delivery tool (Gumroad, Notion, or even a simple membership site) for a powerful creator stack.

**Key features:**
- Discussion spaces organized by topic
- Live streams and events
- Member directory with profiles
- Courses module built into Circle
- Paywalls and member levels
- Bulk member imports
- Zapier integration with other tools

**Best for:** Community-led creators where peer connection is as valuable as course content.

---

### 7. WordPress + LearnDash — Best for Maximum Customization

**Price:** LearnDash from $199/year; WordPress hosting ~$10–$25/month

For creators who want complete control over their course platform, WordPress with the LearnDash LMS plugin provides infinite customization with no transaction fees and no revenue share — ever. The upfront setup is more complex, but the long-term economics are compelling at scale.

**Key features:**
- Full control over design and branding
- No transaction fees or revenue limits
- Advanced quizzes and certificates
- Forum and community (via BuddyBoss or bbPress)
- Complete student data ownership
- WooCommerce for payments (coupons, subscriptions, affiliates)
- Any WordPress plugin for added functionality

**Best for:** Established course creators with technical resources who want maximum control and best economics at scale.

## FAQ

**Which platform has no transaction fees?**
Thinkific (all plans), Kajabi, Podia (Shaker plan), LearnWorlds (Pro+) and WordPress/LearnDash all have zero transaction fees.

**Can I migrate my Teachable courses?**
Course content (videos, PDFs) can be manually transferred. Student enrollments require CSV export and import. Most platforms have migration guides or concierge migration services.

**Which is best for beginners?**
Thinkific (free plan, no fees) or Podia (simple interface, everything included). Both let you start selling without upfront costs or complex setup.

---

## Conclusion

Thinkific is the strongest free Teachable alternative — no transaction fees, unlimited students, one course free forever. Podia wins for creators selling multiple product types who want email marketing built in. Kajabi is the premium choice for serious creators who want to consolidate their entire creator business. And WordPress with LearnDash provides the best long-term economics once you have the audience and technical resources to support it.
