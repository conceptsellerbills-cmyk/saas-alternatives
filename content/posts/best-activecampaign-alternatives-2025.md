# Best ActiveCampaign Alternatives 2025: Cheaper Email Automation Tools

ActiveCampaign is celebrated as one of the most powerful email automation platforms — its visual automation builder, CRM features and deep segmentation capabilities are genuinely best-in-class. But its pricing scales aggressively with contact count: $49/month at 1,000 contacts becomes $149/month at 10,000 and $259/month at 25,000. For growing lists, this becomes a significant business cost.

This guide covers the best ActiveCampaign alternatives for 2025, with particular attention to automation depth, pricing fairness and deliverability.

---

## Why Marketers Look for ActiveCampaign Alternatives

- **Contact-based pricing** that punishes list growth
- **CRM features require Plus plan** ($149/month minimum)
- **Reporting limitations** on the Plus plan
- **Deliverability issues** reported by some users on shared infrastructure
- **Interface complexity** — the power comes with a steep learning curve

---

## Top 7 ActiveCampaign Alternatives for 2025

### 1. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — Best Pricing Model

**Price:** Free (300 emails/day); paid from $25/month (unlimited contacts)

Brevo's volume-based pricing is fundamentally fairer than ActiveCampaign's contact-based model. You pay for emails sent, not contacts stored — which means growing your list doesn't automatically increase your bill.

**Key features:**
- Email marketing with drag-and-drop editor
- Visual marketing automation builder
- CRM with deal pipeline (free)
- SMS marketing and WhatsApp campaigns
- Transactional email via API (SMTP)
- Landing pages and signup forms
- A/B testing on email subject lines and content
- Detailed contact segmentation and scoring

**Best for:** Growing businesses with large lists who find contact-based pricing prohibitive.

---

### 2. Klaviyo — Best for E-commerce Automation

**Price:** Free (500 contacts, 500 emails/month); paid from $20/month

Klaviyo is the dominant email automation platform for e-commerce. Its deep integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce and BigCommerce — including abandoned cart, browse abandonment, post-purchase and win-back flows — are more native and data-rich than ActiveCampaign's e-commerce capabilities.

**Key features:**
- Native Shopify integration with real-time sync
- Pre-built e-commerce automation flows
- SMS marketing alongside email
- Predictive analytics (CLV, churn risk, next purchase)
- Dynamic product recommendations in emails
- Revenue attribution per campaign and automation
- Advanced A/B testing
- 350+ integrations

**Best for:** E-commerce brands using Shopify or WooCommerce that want revenue-attributed automation.

---

### 3. Drip — Best for E-commerce Content Brands

**Price:** From $39/month (2,500 contacts)

Drip combines e-commerce automation with content marketing features, making it popular with DTC brands that have a strong content strategy alongside their sales automation. Its tagging system and workflow builder rival ActiveCampaign's.

**Key features:**
- Visual automation builder with robust branching
- E-commerce revenue attribution
- Custom fields and event tracking
- Shopify, WooCommerce and Magento integration
- SMS alongside email campaigns
- Advanced segmentation by purchase behavior
- Onsite popups and forms

**Best for:** DTC brands with both content marketing and e-commerce automation needs.

---

### 4. ConvertKit — Best for Creators and Bloggers

**Price:** Free (up to 1,000 subscribers); paid from $25/month

ConvertKit (now rebranding to Kit) is the leading email platform for creators — bloggers, podcasters, YouTubers and course creators. Its visual automation builder is simpler than ActiveCampaign's but covers the key creator use cases elegantly.

**Key features:**
- Tag and segment-based subscriber management
- Visual automation builder with simple branching
- Broadcast emails and automated sequences
- Commerce features: sell products and subscriptions directly
- Landing pages and opt-in forms
- Newsletter referral program
- Creator network for audience cross-promotion
- Deliverability reputation for creator content

**Best for:** Bloggers, newsletter writers, podcasters and course creators who want email focused on their audience relationship.

---

### 5. MailerLite — Best Budget Automation Tool

**Price:** Free (1,000 subscribers, 12,000 emails/month); paid from $10/month

MailerLite offers a surprisingly capable automation builder on a free plan — including multi-step automations, website popups, and a landing page builder. The paid plans are among the most affordable in the market.

**Key features:**
- Automation workflows with triggers and conditions
- A/B testing on subject lines, content and send time
- Landing page builder with 40+ templates
- Website popup and embedded forms
- E-commerce integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce)
- Unsubscribe page editor
- Multiple sender addresses
- Advanced reporting (paid)

**Best for:** Small businesses and bloggers that want automation features without ActiveCampaign's price point.

---

### 6. Omnisend — Best for Omnichannel E-commerce

**Price:** Free (500 contacts/month, 500 emails + 60 SMS); paid from $16/month

Omnisend specializes in omnichannel marketing automation for e-commerce — combining email, SMS, push notifications, Facebook Messenger and Google Ads retargeting in a unified automation workflow. No other platform handles this breadth at this price.

**Key features:**
- Email + SMS + push notification automation
- Shopify, BigCommerce, WooCommerce, WixStores
- Pre-built omnichannel automation templates
- Product picker for dynamic content
- Segmentation by purchase history, web activity
- Campaign booster (resend to non-openers automatically)
- Revenue attribution across channels
- A/B testing with automatic winner selection

**Best for:** E-commerce brands that want email, SMS and push notifications coordinated in a single automation.

---

### 7. Mailchimp — Best Known Alternative

**Price:** Free (500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month); paid from $13/month

Mailchimp is the most recognized email marketing platform globally. While it lacks ActiveCampaign's automation depth, it covers the essentials well and integrates with virtually every tool through native connectors.

**Key features:**
- Classic and new automation builder
- AI content generation for subject lines and body
- Audience insights and predicted demographics
- Landing pages and signup forms
- Postcards and social media ads
- E-commerce integrations
- 300+ app integrations

**Best for:** Teams that want a recognized, broadly compatible platform with the largest integration ecosystem.

## FAQ

**Which ActiveCampaign alternative has the best automation?**
Brevo and Drip come closest to ActiveCampaign's automation depth. For e-commerce specifically, Klaviyo and Omnisend offer more revenue-specific automation out of the box.

**Is there a free alternative with automation?**
MailerLite offers multi-step automations on its free plan (1,000 subscribers). Brevo includes basic automation on its free tier.

**Can I migrate my ActiveCampaign automations?**
Contacts, tags and sequences can be exported. Automations must be recreated manually in the new platform. Most providers have migration guides and some offer concierge migration services.

---

## Conclusion

Brevo is the best ActiveCampaign alternative for most businesses — its volume-based pricing removes the contact growth penalty, and its automation builder covers the majority of use cases. For e-commerce, Klaviyo or Omnisend offer more native revenue attribution. ConvertKit is the strongest choice for creators and newsletter writers. And MailerLite provides solid automation at the lowest price point for budget-conscious teams.
