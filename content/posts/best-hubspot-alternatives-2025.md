# Best HubSpot Alternatives 2025: Cheaper CRM & Marketing Tools

HubSpot is one of the most popular all-in-one CRM and marketing platforms in the world — and one of the most expensive. Once you move past the free tier, costs can quickly climb to $800–$3,000+ per month. For startups, small businesses and lean teams, that price tag is simply not justified.

The good news: there are excellent HubSpot alternatives that offer comparable — or even better — functionality at a fraction of the cost. This guide covers the top options for 2025, whether you need a CRM, email automation, landing pages or the full marketing suite.

---

## Why Look for a HubSpot Alternative?

HubSpot's pricing structure is its biggest pain point. The free CRM is genuinely good, but the moment you need automation, reporting or more than 1,000 contacts in email marketing, you're looking at the Starter plan at $50/month minimum — scaling all the way to thousands per month for the Professional and Enterprise tiers.

Other common complaints include:
- **Contact-based pricing** that punishes list growth
- **Features locked behind higher tiers** (A/B testing, custom reporting, predictive lead scoring)
- **Steep onboarding costs** for larger implementations
- **Vendor lock-in** with proprietary data formats

If any of these resonate, you're in the right place.

## Top 8 HubSpot Alternatives for 2025

### 1. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — Best Overall Alternative

**Price:** Free up to 300 emails/day; paid from $25/month (unlimited contacts)

Brevo is arguably the strongest direct HubSpot alternative for small to mid-size businesses. Unlike HubSpot, Brevo charges by email volume — not by contact count — which is a fundamentally fairer pricing model if you have a large list but send infrequently.

**Key features:**
- Email marketing with drag-and-drop editor
- Marketing automation with visual workflow builder
- Built-in CRM (free)
- SMS marketing, live chat, landing pages
- Transactional email via SMTP/API

**Best for:** Businesses with large contact lists that want email + CRM without the HubSpot price tag.

**Migration difficulty:** ⭐⭐☆☆☆ — HubSpot export is straightforward; Brevo imports CSV cleanly.

---

### 2. ActiveCampaign — Best for Advanced Automation

**Price:** From $29/month (1,000 contacts)

ActiveCampaign is widely regarded as the gold standard for email automation depth. Its visual automation builder is more intuitive than HubSpot's and supports more complex conditional logic at lower price points.

**Key features:**
- Advanced automation builder with 850+ triggers and actions
- CRM with pipeline management and deal scoring
- Predictive sending and win probability scoring
- Deep integrations with 900+ apps
- Site tracking and event-based automation

**Best for:** Teams that live inside their automation workflows and need granular control.

**Migration difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ — ActiveCampaign's import wizard handles contacts and tags well; rebuilding automations takes time.

---

### 3. Pipedrive — Best CRM-Focused Alternative

**Price:** From $14.90/user/month

If you primarily use HubSpot as a CRM and find the marketing tools overkill, Pipedrive is the answer. It's built by salespeople, for salespeople — with one of the cleanest pipeline interfaces in the market.

**Key features:**
- Visual drag-and-drop sales pipeline
- Activity reminders and goal tracking
- Email sync with two-way Gmail/Outlook integration
- AI sales assistant with deal insights
- 400+ integrations

**Best for:** Sales teams that want a focused, distraction-free CRM without marketing complexity.

---

### 4. Zoho CRM — Best Value for Full Suite

**Price:** Free for 3 users; paid from $20/user/month

Zoho offers a complete business suite that rivals HubSpot's breadth at a significantly lower price. Zoho CRM sits at the center, but you can add Zoho Campaigns (email marketing), Zoho Desk (support), and Zoho Analytics without the per-module pricing shock.

**Key features:**
- Full CRM with leads, contacts, deals, accounts
- AI assistant (Zia) for predictions and anomaly detection
- Workflow automation and approval processes
- Territory management and quota tracking
- Tight integration across 45+ Zoho apps

**Best for:** Growing businesses that want an integrated suite without paying HubSpot Enterprise prices.

---

### 5. Mailchimp — Best for Email-First Teams

**Price:** Free up to 500 contacts; paid from $13/month

Mailchimp has evolved well beyond email into a basic all-in-one marketing platform. If your primary use case is email marketing with some light CRM features, Mailchimp delivers solid value — especially on the free tier.

**Key features:**
- Email marketing with strong template library
- Audience segmentation and behavioral targeting
- Basic CRM with contact profiles
- Landing pages and forms
- Basic automation (welcome sequences, abandoned cart)

**Best for:** Small businesses and creators who need email-first marketing without CRM complexity.

---

### 6. Mautic — Best Open Source Alternative

**Price:** Free (self-hosted)

Mautic is the most powerful open source marketing automation platform available. It gives you HubSpot-level automation capabilities with zero monthly fees — the only cost is hosting (typically $10–$50/month on a VPS).

**Key features:**
- Full email marketing with automation workflows
- CRM with contact scoring and lifecycle stages
- Landing page and form builder
- Multi-channel campaigns (email, SMS, social)
- Complete data ownership — no vendor lock-in

**Best for:** Technical teams that want full control and are comfortable managing a self-hosted application.

---

### 7. EngageBay — Best Budget All-in-One

**Price:** Free forever plan; paid from $14.99/user/month

EngageBay is specifically designed as a HubSpot alternative and openly markets itself as such. It combines CRM, email marketing, help desk and live chat in a single platform at a dramatically lower price.

**Key features:**
- 360-degree contact view combining marketing and sales
- Email sequences and broadcast campaigns
- Help desk with ticketing and canned responses
- Appointment scheduling built in
- Landing page and form builder

**Best for:** Bootstrapped startups that need the full marketing + sales + support stack.

---

### 8. Salesforce Essentials — Best Enterprise Pivot

**Price:** From $25/user/month

If you're moving away from HubSpot Enterprise because you need more power (not less cost), Salesforce Essentials is worth considering. It's the entry point to the world's most powerful CRM ecosystem.

**Key features:**
- Best-in-class pipeline and opportunity management
- Einstein AI for predictive lead scoring
- AppExchange with 5,000+ integrations
- Advanced reporting and dashboards
- Unlimited customization via Apex and flows

**Best for:** Enterprise teams that have outgrown HubSpot's reporting and customization limits.

## How to Choose the Right HubSpot Alternative

**Choose Brevo if:** You have a large contact list and send marketing emails regularly — the volume-based pricing will save you hundreds per month.

**Choose ActiveCampaign if:** Automation depth is your priority and you run complex multi-step customer journeys.

**Choose Pipedrive if:** Your team is sales-focused and the marketing suite is mostly unused.

**Choose Zoho if:** You want a full business suite and prefer one vendor for everything.

**Choose Mautic if:** You're technical, value data ownership, and want zero licensing fees.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I export my HubSpot data easily?**
Yes. HubSpot allows you to export contacts, companies, deals and activities as CSV files. Most alternatives have direct HubSpot import wizards. The main challenge is rebuilding workflows and sequences in the new platform.

**Is there a free HubSpot alternative with automation?**
Brevo's free plan includes basic automation. Mautic is fully free and self-hosted with advanced automation. EngageBay's free plan includes basic sequences.

**Which HubSpot alternative is best for B2B SaaS?**
ActiveCampaign or Salesforce Essentials. Both handle deal tracking, scoring and complex automation sequences that B2B SaaS teams rely on.

**How long does migration from HubSpot take?**
For a small team (under 5,000 contacts, 5–10 active sequences): 1–2 weeks. For larger implementations: 4–8 weeks including data cleaning, workflow rebuilding and team training.

**Does any alternative match HubSpot's reporting?**
Salesforce comes closest. Zoho Analytics (addon) is also strong. Most SMB alternatives have simpler reporting — which is often sufficient.

## Conclusion

HubSpot is a powerful platform, but its pricing quickly becomes disproportionate for what most businesses actually use. Brevo, ActiveCampaign and Zoho CRM cover 90% of HubSpot's use cases at 20–40% of the cost. For teams comfortable with self-hosting, Mautic eliminates licensing fees entirely. Choose based on your primary need: CRM depth, email automation power, or overall cost reduction.
